Vietnam is a great place to visit for a staggering natural beauty and cultural complexities. Vietnam is probably not the top country to visit but it is very beautiful and filled with delicious food. Vietnam's most distinctive architecture can be seen in temples, which can be found all over the country. For more information about Vietnam's country and history, click here.

Tourist Spots

There are a handfull of destinations to visit in Vietnam, like these for example,

  • Halong Bay
    • - The seascape of Halong Bay is one of the world's most spellbinding sea views. There are plenty of caves in the bay that can be entered including the Hang Sung Sot. The highlight for most people though, is simply cruising and soaking up the changing scenery of pinnacles as you pass by.
  • Ho Chi Minh City
    • - For big city fans, no visit to Vietnam is really complete without a visit to Ho Chi Minh City, the buzzing and crazy commercial hub of the country.
  • Hue
    • - One of Vietnam's most historic towns, is packed tothe brim with relics from the reign of the 19th-century Nguyen emperors. Sitting along the banks of the gorgeous Perfume River, the Imperial Enclosure is a huge site set within walls that sprawl for 2.5 kilometers

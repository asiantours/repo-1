Malaysia

Malaysia is a South-East Asian country, neighbouring Thailand, Cambodia and Indonesia. It has thirteen states and three federeal territories. The official language is Malay, and theur alphabet is latin. The currency is Ringgit(RM). Malaysia lies near the equator, therefore it's climate is warm and wet. Food Trip

Cuisines Description
Nasi lemak Nasi lemak is considered as Malaysia's national food. It's name literally means "oily or fatty rice", but this content really means "rich or creamy". It is a Malay fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaf. Rice is soaked in cocnut cream then the mixture is steamed. It gets its distinctive flavour from the pandan leaves that are cooked with it.
Popiah Popiah is derived from Chinese roots. It is Malaysia's version of spring rolls. A popiah skin is soft, and it is a thin-paper like crepe, made from wheat flour.The filling inside a popoiah varies, but there are either meat or vegetables, such as shrimp and jicama (turnips}.
Rendang Rendang is a spicy meat dish from Indonesia, but Malaysia serves it on their tables as well. There are variations of rendang - chicken rendang, duck rendang, liver rendang, spleen rendang. It is best served in how or room temperature. Many people consider it as a curry, but Indonesia and Malaysia don't consider it as one, because it contains less and is richer.

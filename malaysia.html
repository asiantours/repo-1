Food Trip

Cuisines Description
Nasi lemak Nasi lemak is considered as Malaysia's national food. It is a Malay fragrant rice dish cooked in coconut milk and pandan leaf.
Popiah Popiah is Malaysia's version of spring rolls.
Rendang Rendang is a spicy meat dish. There are variations of rendang - chicken rendang, duck rendang, liver rendang, spleen rendang

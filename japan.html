The Land Of The Rising Sun

Japan is a great place to visit for a wonderful vacation. Japan is an island nation in the Pacific Ocean with many cities, imperial palaces, national parks and thousnads of shrines and temples. The capital of Japan is Tokyo. Japan has an estimate population of 127 million citizens and the currency is japanese yen. People first came to Japanout 30,000 years ago. The first society, called the Jomon culture arose about 12,000 years ago. Around the same time, the Ainu people arrived by boat from Siberia. In 660 B.C., Japan's first emperor who was Jimmu Tenno came to power.

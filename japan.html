The Land Of The Rising Sun

Japan is a great place to visit for a wonderful vacation. Japan is an island nation in the Pacific Ocean with many cities, imperial palaces, national parks and thousnads of shrines and temples. About 19.7 million overseas tourists landd in Japan last year. The capital of Japan is Tokyo. Japan has an estimate population of 127 million citizens and the currency is japanese yen.

Asia's World City

Offically called as "Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic China". Hong Kong is a autonomous territory that used to be a British colony. It is densely and vibrant populated. Hong Kong is very urban, and they are a lot of skyscrapers. Annually, Hong Kong is also a major shopping destination, famed for bespoke tailors and Temple Street Night Market. Hongkong receives 70 million tourists.

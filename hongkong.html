Asia's World City

Offically called as "Hong Kong Special Administrative Region of the People's Republic China". Hong Kong is a autonomous territory that used to be a British colony. It is densely and vibrant populated. Hong Kong is very urban, and they are a lot of skyscrapers. Annually, Hong Kong is also a major shopping destination, famed for bespoke tailors and Temple Street Night Market. Currency in Hong Kong is Hongkong dollars. It is considered as the world's forth population densed region. They receive 70 million tourists. The diaclect spoken is Cantonese, and because of Bristish influence, the people of Hong Kong also speak English. Food Trip

Cuisines Description
Congee Congee is that dish a mother serves to their child when they are sick. However, even if you aren't sick but looking for a warm meal, congee is perfect on a cold day. Congee is rice porridge that can be served with additional and side dishes. You can put almost anything in a congee - meat, fish and flavourings.
Roast Goose The name says it all, but don't worry or be grossed out. Roast goose is a delicacy usually cooked in charcoal ovens. It takes 45 minutes for it to roast, and the outcome is beautiful. The meat is somewhat sweet, and can be paired with different food.
Cha Siu Bao Cha siu bao is a bun filled with barbeque-flavoured char siu pork. " They are served as a type of dim sum and are sometimes sold in Chinese bakeries.

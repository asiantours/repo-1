The Pearl of the Orient

The Philippines, a small nation with 7, 641 islands, is diverse in cultural heritage and nature. About 3.5 million tourists travel in the country every year, visiting infamous destinations, such as beaches, mountains, diving spots, islands and rainforests. Filipinos speak Tagalog, as their main dialect, and English, as the second language. To learn more about Philippines, click here.
